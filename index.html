RUNWAY FOR HOPE

Our Story

Runway for Hope was founded in 2023 with a mission inspired by the deep impact cancer has on loved ones. Witnessing the emotional and physical toll of pediatric cancer, we set out to bring joy, empowerment, and support to young fighters and their families.

What began as a simple initiative to uplift children through toy drives and support cards has grown into a full-scale nonprofit, aiming to host annual fashion shows where pediatric cancer warriors shine as models. Through our work, we honor the resilience of these children and strive to bring light to their journey.

Our Impact

So far, Runway for Hope has raised over $3,000 cumulatively and collaborated with two hospitals—Rutgers Health Cancer Institute and Hackensack Meridian Health's Joseph M. Sanzari Children’s Hospital. We are currently in contact with three more hospitals to expand our impact and bring joy to even more pediatric cancer patients.

Our Vision

Runway for Hope is a nonprofit dedicated to supporting pediatric cancer patients through toy drives, personalized support cards, and an annual fashion show where young fighters take the spotlight as models.

By working closely with hospitals and families, we aim to bring joy, empowerment, and a sense of community to children battling cancer. Our mission is to provide hope, celebrate strength, and create unforgettable moments for these brave kids.
